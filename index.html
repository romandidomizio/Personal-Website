My path into technology started with a Computer Science degree from CU Boulder. AI in Action

This integrated view naturally led me to artificial intelligence. As a Technical Product Manager Intern at The Verse, I am immersed in AI product strategy for an emotionally intelligent therapeutic platform. My responsibilities include architecting AI roadmaps from the ground up and developing innovative LLM simulation systems. This role is very hands on, involving direct work with Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG), orchestrating multi agent systems using tools like LangGraph, and diving into techniques such as RLHF and LoRA/QLoRA fine tuning to build agents with high emotional intelligence. My focus is on creating dynamic systems that not only interact but also learn, adapt, and truly comprehend the context of their decisions. This often involves close collaboration with engineering and research teams to bring these complex systems to life.
