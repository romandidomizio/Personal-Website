AI in Action

This perspective is the foundation of my work in artificial intelligence. At The Verse, I lead the AI initiative for WalkXR, a gamified therapeutic platform. My role is to own the product vision and technical strategy, translating the concept of an emotionally intelligent companion into a concrete agentic framework.

This means architecting the entire system. I designed the product roadmap to scale from a single agent MVP to a multi agent OS. I also architected and built a novel LLM simulation system to generate the synthetic data we need for effective agent training and evaluation. It's a hands on leadership role where I define the core technical strategy for our OS, using tools like LangGraph for multi agent orchestration and RAG for knowledge grounding, while also prototyping the foundational agent capabilities myself.

I see a direct line from my experience with multi agent systems to the complex challenges in aerospace and defense, such as autonomous navigation, intelligent reconnaissance, and sophisticated human AI teaming.

My goal is to apply my skills in AI strategy and systems architecture to these critical domains. I am pursuing roles where I can contribute to the entire lifecycle of these technologies, from initial design and prototyping to product management and deployment. Whether as a software engineer building the core systems or as a product manager guiding the strategic vision, my focus remains the same: to build robust, reliable, and impactful autonomous systems.
